# Alt Text Guide for Communications Teams

## Good alt text answers the content purpose

Do not describe every visual detail. Describe what the user needs to know from the image in context.

## Examples

### Simple photo

Poor: `people at table`

Better: `Panelists answer audience questions during the applicant webinar.`

### Chart

Poor: `bar chart`

Better: `Bar chart showing applications increased each year from 2022 to 2024, with the largest increase in 2024.`

### Logo

Poor: `image`

Better: `Decks2Web logo`

### Decorative divider

Use empty alt text or mark decorative:

```html
<img src="divider.svg" alt="">
```

## Human approval rule

AI can draft alt text, but a human should approve alt text for:

- Scientific figures
- Public health guidance
- Charts/data visualizations
- Infographics
- Medical diagrams
- Maps
- Anything where accuracy matters
